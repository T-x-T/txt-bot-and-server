How to use the Nether Coords data pack?

When in Overworld: /trigger nc_inOverworld to get the Nether coords.
When in Nether: /trigger nc_inNether to get the Overworld coords.

How to found a town?

1) Be on Paxterya for more than one week.
2) Gather 5 citizens.
3) DM TxT on Discord with your town name, its location, mayor and citizens.
4) If you're not too close to another town, your town will be announced on Discord and you'll get a Dynmap marker.

Note: If all citizens of your town haven't been online for more than 30 days, the town status will be revoked.

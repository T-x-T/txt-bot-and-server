The Paxterya world currently consists of three maps:

  • The Vanilla Map

    • Spawn point when first joining
    • 500x500 blocks around spawn are reserved for medieval buildings
    • Protectable plots
    • /warp vanilla

  • The Farming Map

    • Large biomes
    • Preferred place for big and ugly farms
    • Protectable plots
    • /warp farming

  • The Adventure Map

    • Custom terrain
    • Preferred place for your most beautiful buildings
    • Building of big farms not wished
    • Protectable plots
    • /warp adventure

The center of Paxterya is the Spawn Hub on the Vanilla Map. 500x500 blocks around the Spawn Hub, there is a build-protected area. You can however get a plot there to add a medieval-style building. Like that, we hope to create a beautiful medieval city around the Spawn Hub!
Outside of the spawn area, you can build as you wish.
